About
The Heartbreak Collective is Droboard's largest genre community — a space for readers and writers who find meaning in the complicated space between love and loss. We share stories, confessions, polls and recommendations. This is a safe space. Be kind.
Community Rules
1
Be kind. No personal attacks, shaming, or mockery.
2
No spoilers without a warning tag.
3
Story recommendations must include the author's name.
4
Keep posts related to heartbreak, betrayal, or emotional fiction.
5
Confessions are posted with care. Don't share someone else's story.
